1. Introduction This platform facilitates research data donation from participants to Aalto University researchers. By using this platform, you agree to the following terms and conditions. Participants understand their data will be used for academic research as described in the Privacy Statement Researchers agree to use the donated data solely for research purposes and in accordance with ethical guidelines. Please note that Aalto may occasionally change these guidelines due to service development. Any updates will be communicated by updating the aalto.fi page. 2. Eligibility The data donation platform is available to participants in research studies conducted by Aalto University researchers. If you are under the age of 16 you confirm that you have your guardian's consent to use this service. Researchers must be affiliated with Aalto University or approved partner institutions. 3. Data Use and Restrictions 3.1 Permitted Uses: Researchers may use participant data for any legitimate academic research, including research not specified at enrollment. 3.2 Prohibited Uses: Researchers may not commercially exploit, sell, or redistribute participant personal data. 4. Data Storage & Retention Refer to Privacy Statement for data retention periods and deletion procedures. Upon participant withdrawal, data collection ceases immediately and identifiers are deleted within 30 days. 5. Participant Withdrawal Participants may withdraw from any study at any time. Withdrawal stops data collection immediately and triggers data deletion per Privacy Statement timelines. Contact Aalto RSEs (rse@aalto.fi) for assistance in withdrawing from studies. 6. Researcher Obligations Researchers agree to process data only for approved research purposes and to comply with Aalto's research ethics policies and GDPR. Researchers agree to institutional oversight and data auditing. 7. Compliance and Enforcement Aalto University reserves the right to revoke researcher access or terminate data collection for violations of these terms or research ethics policies. 8. Contact Information Contact Aalto RSEs (rse@aalto.fi) for more information or assistance regarding these terms of service.
